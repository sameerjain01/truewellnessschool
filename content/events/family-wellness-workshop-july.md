Join True Wellness for a hands-on family wellness workshop at the Fairfax County Library. Parents and children will learn together, build healthy habits, and walk away with a simple family wellness plan they can start using the same day.

**What to expect:**
- Introduction to the Four Pillars of Wellness
- Simple stress management tools for kids and parents
- Practical nutrition tips for busy family life
- Family wellness goal-setting activity
- Free resources to take home

This event is free and open to the community.
