Join the True Wellness team at a free community wellness fair open to all ages. Whether you're just starting your wellness journey or looking to deepen your practice, this event has something for everyone.

**What's included:**
- Free Four Pillars Wellness Assessment stations
- Guided meditation and breathwork demonstrations
- Family wellness activity stations for children
- Information tables on True Wellness programs
- Free wellness resource packets
- Healthy snacks and refreshments

This is a free, open-to-the-public event. Bring your family, friends, and neighbors.
