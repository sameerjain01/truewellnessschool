## Our Promise

We're True Wellness. We believe in a simpler life — and that extends to how we treat your data.

**We don't track you. We don't sell your data. We don't use advertising cookies. Ever.**

This isn't a legal technicality. It's who we are.

---

## What We Collect

### Information you give us

When you fill out a contact, partnership, or assessment form, we collect the information you provide (name, email, phone, message). This goes directly into a private Google Sheet and is used only to respond to your inquiry.

### Nothing else

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other behavioral tracking tools. We do not place advertising cookies on your device.

---

## What We Store Locally (on Your Device)

We use **localStorage** — not cookies — to remember your display preferences:

- **Text size** — the A−/A/A+ setting you choose in the accessibility bar
- **Dark mode** — whether you've enabled dark mode

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us.
