## Delivery Options

True Wellness corporate programs are available as:

- **Lunch & Learns** — 45–60 minute educational sessions on wellness topics
- **Half-Day Workshops** — Immersive learning experiences for teams
- **Quarterly Wellness Days** — Full-day wellness events for your organization
- **Ongoing Wellness Challenges** — Month-long team engagement programs
- **Mindful Leadership Sessions** — Focused sessions for managers and executives
- **Resilience Training Series** — Multi-session programs for sustained wellbeing

All programs can be delivered onsite, virtually, or as a hybrid.
