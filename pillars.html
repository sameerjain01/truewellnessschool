Framework

The 6 Pillars of
True Wellness

Inspired by Dr. Frank Lipman — a complete, science-backed framework for mastering wellness that goes far beyond any single habit or supplement.

Wellness isn't a single action — it's a system. When all six pillars are tended to, the body heals, the mind clears, and life feels like something you're living rather than enduring. Neglect even one, and the others suffer.

"Wellness isn't a trend — it's a way of life. This makes it simple, actionable, and life-changing."

😴

Sleep

Pillar One

Protect Your Rest. It Heals Everything.

Sleep is not a luxury — it's the single most powerful recovery tool you have. During deep sleep, your brain clears metabolic waste, your cells regenerate, hormones rebalance, and memories consolidate. No supplement, no workout, no diet compensates for consistent sleep deprivation.

7–9 hoursThe optimal window for most adults to achieve full cellular repair.
Dark & coolYour bedroom environment directly controls sleep hormone production.
No screensBlue light suppresses melatonin — protect the 90 minutes before bed.
Consistent rhythmSame wake time daily is more powerful than any sleep supplement.
🥗

Eat

Pillar Two

Food Is Medicine. Choose Wisely.

Every meal is a message to your cells. A plant-rich, whole-food diet nourishes your microbiome, reduces inflammation, stabilises blood sugar, and gives your brain the fuel it needs to stay sharp. You are not just what you eat — you are what you absorb.

Microbiome firstNourish your gut bacteria — they run your immunity, mood, and metabolism.
Whole foodsThe fewer ingredients on the label, the better it is for your body.
PhytonutrientsColour on your plate = antioxidant protection for every cell.
HydrationMost fatigue, brain fog, and headaches are dehydration in disguise.
🤸

Move

Pillar Three

Motion Is Lotion. Stay Fluid.

Movement is not just physical — it is neurological. Exercise grows new brain cells, regulates mood, strengthens joints, improves sleep, and extends lifespan. The goal is not peak performance, it's a body that serves you for decades without pain.

Strength trainingEssential for bone density, metabolism, and hormonal health after 30.
Mobility & stretchingFlexibility preserves joint health and prevents chronic pain.
Daily walking10,000 steps daily reduces all-cause mortality risk dramatically.
Breath & postureHow you carry your body shapes your energy and confidence.
🛡️

Protect

Pillar Four

Detox Daily, Not Just Sometimes.

We live in a toxic world — from processed food to pollutants to digital overload. Protecting your body means reducing the daily toxic load so your liver, gut, and immune system can focus on healing rather than just surviving.

Clean productsSwap toxic personal care and household products for natural alternatives.
Reduce processed foodAdditives, seed oils, and ultra-processed ingredients are slow poisons.
Digital detoxYour nervous system needs time away from screens and notifications.
Liver supportHydration, cruciferous vegetables, and herbs support daily detox pathways.
🧘

Unwind

Pillar Five

Your Mind Needs Daily Peace.

Chronic stress is the silent driver of most modern disease — from heart disease to autoimmunity. Your mind needs daily peace, not occasional holidays. Building restoration into every day is not self-indulgence. It is fundamental medicine.

MeditationEven 10 minutes daily measurably rewires the brain for calm and focus.
BreathworkConscious breathing directly activates the parasympathetic nervous system.
Nature timeForest bathing (Shinrin-yoku) lowers cortisol and boosts immunity measurably.
Creative practiceHobbies, art, and music activate the brain's default-mode healing network.
🤝

Connect

Pillar Six

Community Is Medicine Too.

Loneliness is as dangerous as smoking 15 cigarettes a day — that is not a metaphor, it is science. Belonging, meaningful relationships, and community are biological needs. Your tribe heals you in ways nothing else can.

Your tribeFind people who share your values and lift your energy, not drain it.
Deep conversationSurface connection is not enough — vulnerability heals.
ServiceGiving to others activates the brain's reward circuit and extends lifespan.
BoundariesTrue connection requires knowing what to protect and what to let in.
