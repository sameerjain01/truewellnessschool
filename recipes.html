The Green Powerhouse Smoothie

The Wellness Sip

Fueling Body, Mind & Soul

🥬

Kale

1 cup

🌿

Spinach

1 cup

🥑

Avocado

1 whole

🥦

Broccoli

½ cup

🍏

Green Apple

½

🫐

Berries

½ cup

🍋 Lemon juice 🥄 Apple cider vinegar 🧂 Sea salt
🌱 Plant-Based 💚 Anti-Inflammatory 🧠 Brain Food

This blend delivers a rich mix of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to energise your body and support cellular health. Creamy avocado brings heart-healthy fats, green apple adds refreshing sweetness, and mixed berries infuse immune-boosting vitamin C and phytonutrients.

How to make it

  1. Add kale and spinach to blender first
  2. Add avocado, broccoli, and green apple
  3. Add berries, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar
  4. Pinch of sea salt + ½ cup cold water
  5. Blend on high 60 seconds until silky smooth
  6. Serve immediately — best fresh
