Green Powerhouse Smoothie

This blend delivers a rich mix of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to energise your body and support cellular health. Creamy avocado brings heart-healthy fats, green apple adds refreshing sweetness, and mixed berries infuse immune-boosting vitamin C and phytonutrients.

How to make it

  1. Add kale and spinach to blender first (bottom layer)
  2. Add avocado, broccoli florets, and green apple
  3. Pour in mixed berries, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar
  4. Add a pinch of sea salt and ½ cup cold water
  5. Blend on high for 60 seconds until silky smooth
  6. Serve immediately — best fresh
